About Company

Water Treatment
Sales & Services

The idea was floated in 1990 to launch a professional company specializing exclusively in water and environmental services to tap the expected growth and demand in this sector.
The company started by serving the basic water treatment needs of industrial consumers, including RO Plants, Filters, Softeners, De-Ionizers, Chemicals for Boiler & Cooling Circuits, and Ion Exchange Resin.
Over the past 20+ years, the company has excelled in Reverse Osmosis Desalination and recently introduced a new concept for treating and recycling textile effluent, helping address environmental issues and meeting the expectations of foreign buyers of textile products.
Utilizing the latest technologies like SBRs and MBRs in industrial effluent treatment, Absolute Chemical has become a leading supplier of Reverse Osmosis Systems, boasting 100% customer satisfaction and an installed desalination capacity of over 10 MGD.
Absolute Chemical is ISO 9001:2015 Certified, demonstrating customer focus and commitment to accelerating development to offer the best products and professional services economically for the benefit of commercial and industrial consumers' water and wastewater treatment needs.

Max Benjamin
Founder

  • Our Vision

    With the industrial and manufacturing sectors growing, the demand for water in these applications is rising alarmingly, worsened by depleting natural resources. Environmental pollution from untreated industrial waste highlights the need for effluent treatment facilities. Absolute Chemical, a leading water and wastewater treatment company, is well-equipped to meet this growing market demand.

  • Our Mission

    To meet the ever-rising demand for industrial water and wastewater treatment and recycling with the best professional and cost effective approach. Our efforts will be targeted to establish.
